Ukrainian Carpathians
Within different regional and national traditions, there are several overlapping variants of divisions and designations
for various Eastern Carpathian mountain ranges.
Within the wider scope of the Ukrainian Carpathians, a section of the Outer Eastern Carpathians spaning southeastern corner
of Poland, northeastern corner of Slovakia
and western part of Ukraine is commonly known as the Eastern Beskids (Ukrainian: Східні Бескиди; Polish: Beskidy Wschodnie),
while in Slovakia the term Meadowed Mountains
(Slovak: Poloniny) is also used for the same mountain range.
